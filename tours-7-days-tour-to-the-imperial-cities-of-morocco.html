7 Days Tour Visit Historical Imperial Cities Of Morocco Monuments And Architecture Of Morocco:

The Imperial Cities of Morocco are old Capitals of one and more Dynasties. Such as Fes which was the Capital of Morocco several times. Marrakech that was Capital of Saadienes, Meknes was at one the time Capital of Alaouites dynasty. Rabat is the current Capital of Morocco.

Day 1 : Marrakech :

Arrival to the Airport you will find our driver who will pick up you and take you to the hotel or Riad

Day 2 : Guided tour :

After breakfast you have full day will which to visit Marrakech medina (old city) Palaces, Jewish quarter , Saadian tombs, Koutoubia mosque, Ben Youssef Madrasa and the museum, Jamaa lafna (Square The yard at the heart of Marrakesh, where they found the city center pilgrimage residents and visitors and they use a place for meetings. And where there are popular Story-Tellers, snake-charmers, fortune-readers, acrobats, musicians, dancers, models,). Also a tour of the Ramparts and Gardens

Day 3 : Marrakech To Rabat Via Casablanca :

Departure from the hotel or Riad to Rabat via Casablanca. In Casablanca you will visit Hassan 2 mosque (second mosque in the world) and Cornish, have lunch in Casablanca. After taking the way to Rabat .In Rabat you will visit the lovely garden in Morocco names(chella” garden), Mausoleum of Mohamed V, Hassan tower and Kasbah Oudaya

Day 4 : Rabat - Meknes - Fes :

Depart from Rabat to Meknes, and we will visit the Roman ruins of (Volubilis ) and Moulay Idriss town. (Bab Mansour) and Hedim (square) (place Hedim) have lunching Meknes Then Fes. Dinner at the hotel/Riad

Day 5 : Guided Tour Fes :

After breakfast you will begin our guided tour of Fes medina, one of the world’s largest walled in cities. You will explore the narrow streets lined with fresh fruit, mounds of spices, intricately woven Berber carpets and many other art objects of Morocco. A visit to the King’s Palace, the tanneries, and potters are just a few places you will experience. For lunch you may choose to eat in a Moroccan restaurant in the heart of the medina. After lunch you will continue the tour and return to your hotel/Riad in the evening

Day 6 : Fes - Marrakech - Middle Atlas Mountains - Full Day Drive :

After breakfast you leave to Marrakech passing the Middle Atlas Mountains, eleven in Marrakech, you will check into your hotel / Riad and head off to the Djemaa el-Fna (square) Medina. The famous Djemaa el-Fna, is one of the center attractions of Marrakech. Coming alive at night with the local master storytellers, Reviews entertainers, and mouth-watering, the square is well worth the visit. A quiet cup of mint tea in some terrace provides a spectacular aerial view of the bustling square.

Day 7 : Transfer To The Airport Of Marrakech :

After breakfast, transfer to the Airport and depending departure time. Flight back home
