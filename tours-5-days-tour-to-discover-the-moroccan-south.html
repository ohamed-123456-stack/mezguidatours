5 Days Tour From Marrakech Great Southern Morocco To Discover The Moroccan South:


Day 1 : Marrakech - Telouet - Ait Ben Haddou - Ouarzazate :

Departure in the morning from Marrakech at 08h00 to the mountain chain of the High Atlas. Admiring its deep valleys and the peaks of mountains that stretch into the sky, will arrive at the village of Telouet. Here we will visit one of the most beautiful Kasbahs in south of Morocco, one of the Pasha Glaoui that will offer you a wonderful surprise. After the visit we will have lunch, not far from the Kasbah, at Moroccan restaurant where they serve a specialty: the fig tagine with almonds, walnuts and dates, typical of this area. We shall therefore to Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah other wonderful behind a hill. We will also visit this wasting then through its narrow streets, shops and stores. Arrive to Ouarzazate in the evening and spend the night in a Riad

Day 2 : Ouarzazate - Agdez - Tamnougalt – Drra Valley - Zagora :

After breakfast depart for Tamnougalt. Passing Agdez, which marks the beginning of the "Valley of the Thousand Kasbahs", will arrive in Zagora where it terminates itself. A Tamnougalt, waiting for lunch, you can visit this ancient kasbah with dungeons and its beautiful palm trees. After lunch we travel along road for miles along the beautiful Draa Valley. We'll stop at scenic spots from where you can admire the beauty. We will arrive in the evening to Zagora. Night in Riad

Day 3 : Zagora - Tamgrout - Kasbah Ait Isfoul - Mhamid - Erg Chegaga Desert :

After breakfast we leave the beautiful town of Zagora (which is door of the Sahara) . We leave to the discovery a lost treasure in the village of Tamgroute where we will visit the alther Koranic library of Zaouïa and underground Kasbah "Ait Isfoul." This village is also known for the craftsmanship of ceramics. We will resume the journey to M’hamid (the door of the desert) and have lunch. In the afternoon we will resume the sandy path to delve into the most savage desert of southern Morocco. Before reaching the dunes of Chegaga we cross the rocky desert, along way, we stop at oasis in the mountains of Jbel Bani. The dunes will be waiting at sunset, and Over Night in nomadic Tent.

Day 4 : Erg Chegaga - The lake Iriki - Foum Zguid - Taznakht - Ait Ben Haddou :

We have to wake up early if you want to admire the bright lights that give life in Sahara desert. After breakfast, to arrive at Foum Zguid, we will cross dry Lake Iriqui and high deserts whose boundless horizons are adorned with characteristic acacia trees. Have a picnic in oasis before the next step: the village of Taznakht. Famous for the production of Berber carpets, we will have the opportunity to visit the cooperative of women who work at the loom. In the evening we will be in Ait Ben Haddou where we will spend the night in Riad

Day 5 : Ait Ben Haddou - Marrakech :

Consumed breakfast we walk from the Riad Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou crossings of the river. In the summer it is dry, but during the winter, when the water will flow back, cross it hopped on the sandbags placed between one bank and another. E 'can also reach the kasbah via a bridge, but it's less fun! A surround shops and workshops of the local people who will offer rugs, necklaces, turbans and ceramics. In the afternoon back to Marrakech.
