Fez To Fes Desert Tour - 2 Days / 1 Night Via Erg Chebbi Dunes:

This Trip From Fez To Fes Sahara Desert Tours 2Days / 1Night allows you to Travel to the deep south of Morocco and to Experience a Camel Rid Tour over the fabulous Dunes to reach the Camp in the middle of Sahara desert where you will spend the night in a Berber tents made of hair of Camels and goats. In These 2 Days you will enjoy the scenery of the Middle Atlas, view the cedar forest and Wild monkeys (barbary apes), see the Nomads living in tents, Explore the Gorge of Ziz along with its Valley. Get to know how they work with fossils in Erfoud and more than that to taste the best quality of Dates. Hurry up to book your seat in this short Trip from Fez to Merzouga and Erg Chebbi Dunes...

Day 1 : Fes - Ifrane - Azrou - Midelt - Ziz Valley - Merzouga Dunes :

Pick up from your hotel or Riad in Fes very earlier and departure for the south of Morocco. You will have a stop in Ifrane, known as the Switzerland city of Morocco to enjoy, its beauty and to have a memorable pictures. After that you will head toward Azrou to see the monkeys in the cedar forest. Continuing to Midelt for a lunch meal in Moroccan restaurant. In the afternoon, we will cross the pass of TIZI N TALGHAMT to reach Errachidia while admiring the views of Ziz gorges.you will have a stop for a panoramic view of Ziz valley. The driver continues to Erg Chebbi Dunes passing by Erfoud. (the last is famous with fossils and dates) where you may want to visit a local fossil craft workshop. After that you will continue to the captivating dunes of Erg Chebbi Merzouga Dunes. Arrive to the hotel and get ready for a camel trek to enjoy the sunset and sunrise. In the back of your dromedary, you will continue to the camp for overnight in traditional nomad tent. Dinner and spend the night in a bivouac middle of height dunes 250 M

Day 2 : Merzouga Erg Chebbi Dunes - Fez :

Wake up early morning to enjoy the sunrise in the top of the dune in Sahara Desert, come back to your hotel then have your breakfast and shower. After these beautiful moments, you will drive back with same way to Fez and Drop you of on your accommodation in Fez, the end of your short Trip to Morocco
