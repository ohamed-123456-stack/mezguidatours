3 Days Desert trip from Marrakech to Fes via High Atlas mountain,Ait Ben Haddou, Berber villages in Morocco:

This Tour From Marrakech To Fes Via Erg Chebbi 3 Days/2 Nights Desert Tours Camel Treks takes you on a breathtaking expedition in the south of Morocco through the Mountains of high Atlas with its Berber villages , Kasbahs, Gorge, Canyon, Palm Groves, Sahara desert (Merzouga dunes), Middle Atlas Mountains and Cedar forest.

Day 1 : Marrakech - Tizi N’tichka Pass - Ait Benhaddou - Ouarzazate - Kelaa M’gouna - Boumalne Du Dades :

We pick you up from your Hotel/Riad in Marrakech, and departure to Ait Benhaddou over the mountains of high Atlas passing through the Tizi N’tichka pass (2260M), visit the Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou and stop for lunch in a Moroccan restaurant.In the Afternoon we will drive to Ouarzazate and stop near the studios for Pictures then you continue to the city center for a panoramic view of Taourirt kasbah . After that you will head to Kalaa M’gouna and Dades gorge for a night. Dinner and overnight at the hotel/Riad.

Day 2 : Boumalne Dades - Todra Gorges - Tinejdad - Erfoud - Erg Chebbi (Merzouga) :

After breakfast in the hotel, driving towards Tineghir passing by Boumalne du Dades and stopping for a panoramic view of dades valley.and we will continue to Todra gorges via Tineghir. Stopping along the way for photos and admiring the views. Arrival to Todra Gorges to enjoy the marvelous Canyons views. In the afternoon we continue to Tinejdad and have a lunch then to Erfoud where you will visit a local fossil craft workshop. After that you will continue to the captivating Send dunes of Erg Chebbi, Arrive to the hotel and get ready for a camel trek to enjoy the sunset and sunrise. And you will continue to the camp for an overnight in nomad tent. Dinner and spend the night in a bivouac in the middle of the dunes

Day 3 : Merzouga - Erfoud - Errachidia - Midelt - Azrou - Ifrane - Fes :

Wake up early morning to enjoy the sunrise on the top of the dune in Sahara Desert then have your breakfast, then you will ride your camel back to the hotel where you can shower. And You will depart to Errachidia via Erfoud, Aoufous and Ziz valley then stopping for a panoramic views of the oasis of Aoufous of Tafilalte region. Continuation to Midelt across the pass of Tizi N’talghamt and have lunch. After lunch you will be driven to Fes via the Cedar forest in Azrou and the mountain of the middle Atlas with the possibility to see the monkey. You may have a stop in Ifrane (the Switzerland City Morocco) before arriving to Fez, drop you of in your Hotel or Riad in Fez.
