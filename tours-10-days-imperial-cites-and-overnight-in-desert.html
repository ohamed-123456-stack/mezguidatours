Camel Ride In Merzouga And Night in Desert - 10 Days Casablanca Desert Trip:


Day 1 : Arrival Casablanca Airport :

Reception, assistance and transfer to Hotel at Casablanca, Dinner and overnight

Day 2 : Casablanca via Rabat :

Visit one of the best highlights of Casablanca Hassan II mosque, is the second mosque in the world then departure to the imperial city Rabat. Visit the ramparts and walls surrounding the Royal Palace, the Kasbah of Oudaya, the Mausoleum of Mohamed V and the Hassan II. accommodations at the hotel

Day 3 : Rabat - Fez :

After the breakfast continue to Visit the Medina of Meknes. Then Visiting the Roman ruin of Volubilis and the Jewish quarter. Spend the night at the Hotel/Riad in Fez

Day 4 : Fez Guided tour :

This day is dedicated to Fez, the oldest imperial city in Morocco, founded in the 8th century by Moulay Idriss II. You will visit the Medina, Medersas Attarine, Fontaine Ennajarine, Mausoleum of Moulay Idriss and the Karawiyin mosque. You will also visit the new medina of Fes Jdid. In the evening we will return to the hotel/Riad after a very busy sightseeing day.

Day 5 : Fez Via Merzouga :

Long scenic drive to the desert of Merzouga (Erg chebbi). Drive through Azrou Cedar Forest, Ziz Velley and gorges, and Erfoud via erg chebbi dunes where we Take a camel ride deep into the Sahara Desert to spent the night under the stars or at the bivouac (nomadic tents).

Day 6 : Merzouga Via Todra Gorge Dades Gorge Roses Valley :

Wake up for the beautiful sunrise over the dunes of Erg Chebbi. Camel trek back to the Hotel. Traveling to the Todra gorge, then to Dades Gorge and spend the night

Day 7 : Dades Gorge via Ouarzazate – Ait Benhadou Kasnah- Marrakech

After breakfast starting our journey, we take the road of a thousand Kasbahs to Quazazate (Hollywood city in Morocco) where you will visit the Kasbah of Taourirt and the famous kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou sitting in the nearby mountains. Set off through the Tizi N'tichka Pass over the southern Atlas Mountains to the touristique town in Morocco of Marrakech. Spend the night in a hotel/Riad

Day 8 : Marrakech - Guided tour :

Marrakech founded in 1062 has much to offer starting with the Menara Gardens, Koutoubia Mosque, Saadien tombs and the Bahia Palace the Madrassa ben youssef named (Koranic school) . Then to Jamaa El Fna (square) where all the souks (marketplace) will keep you busy until the evening entertainment begins. Back to your hotel/Riad

Day 9 : Marrakech - Casablanca :

Free morning in Marrakech to visit your favorite places. Departures to Casablanca in the afternoon to your Hotel.

Day 10 : Casablanca - Airport :

Transfer to the airport.
