8 Days Tour Atlantic Coast of Morocco :


Day 1 : Marrakech :

Greeting at the airport and meet our driver then transfer to Hotel or Riyad.

Day 2 : Marrakech - Tizi-n-test- Taroudant (223 km)

After breakfast, we’re going to drive towards Taroudant through the Tizi-n-Test pass (2100 m high) where you are going to enjoy the beautiful landscape from the pass. Lunch will be there in some places, then we’re going to continue to Taroudant. After getting this fascinate city we will going for Tour to explore this wonderful city with his walls. Overnight and dinner in Hotel Riyad.

Day 3 : Taroudante - Tafraoute - Kerdous Pass (220 km)

After breakfast in your accomodation, departure along a secondary road across the Anti Atlas (1974 M high) by crossing of many berber villages, our Driver is ready to stop whatever and wherever for photos and toilettes. After long drive we’re going to stop for lunch in some of Morocain restaurants there. After lunch time we continue to Tafraoute the valley of colours, free time for some walk and taking photos. Dinner and accommodation in Riyad Hotel.

Day 4 : Kerdous Pass- Bou Izakarn - Goulmime - Tiznit (227 km)

After breakfast, we’re going to drive through the Kerdous pass. After crossing the pass we continue towards this typical place which is Goulimine. Lunch on the seafront of Aglou. Arrival to one of the beautiful and magic cities in the south of Morocco which is Tiznit. Overnight & Dinner in hotel.

Day 5 : Tiznit - Agadir - Essaouira (238 km)

After breakfast, departure along the Atlantic coast towards Agadir the capital of sous plain and the ambassador of the vegetables and fruits in Morocco. Lunch and tour of the city, then we continue towards the peace of Essaouira city here where we’re going to explore the old medina and juish city not only but also the mean port of the city. The overnight & Dinner in Hotel Riyad.

Day 6 : Essaouira - Marrakech (174 km)

After breakfast, free morning to visit Essaouira. Lunch will be in front of the sea in one of the traditional restaurants with fish menu.after lunch departure towards Marrakech via Chichaoua and we have the possibility of break in one of the feminine Argan cooperatives to get some information about the Moroccan Argan oil Overnight & dinner in Riyad Hotel.

Day 7 : Marrakech - Guided tour

After breakfast, historic visit of Marrakech this time by the city guide to get in touch with culture and authenticity of Marrakech. Lunch will be in Jamea El fanna(the Mean Square). Tour around the souks and old medina. Dinner out in one of the places in Jamea Elfanna. Overnight in Riyad Hotel

Day 8 : Marrakech

After breakfast, transfer to airport depend of the flight back home.
