2 Days trip from Marrakech to Zagora Desert Camel trek in 2 days 1 night


Day 1 : Marrakech - Ouarzazate - Zagora - Camel ride night in nomad tent :

We will drive over the High Atlas Mountains through Tizi n’Tichka ( 2260 m). On the side of the mountains, you will see Berber and traditional villages until we arrive at Ouarzazate “the Hollywood city of Morocco” stop for lunch and before we continue to Zagora desert visit of Taourirt Kasbah, after we arrive to Agdz and drive nearby Dra’a Valley rich of date palm trees. We will continue to Zagora where your camels are waiting for you to start your camel trip about 2hrs, then spend the night in a nomad tent

Day 2 : Zagora - Ouarzazate And Ait benhaddou Kasbah Marrakech :

After seeing the sunrise we go back on the camels, after breakfast and having a shower, we will start our journey back to Marrakech, on the way we will visit ait Benhaddou Kasbah,after visiting and having lunch we will continue our way to Cross the High Atlas Mountains via the highest road pass in Morocco (Tizni Tichka 2260 M), offering impressive Lund skype of valleys, mountains and many Berber villages nearby. Arrive in Marrakech evening.
