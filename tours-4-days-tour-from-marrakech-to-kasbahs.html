4 Days Desert Tour from Marrakech - Marrakech to Merzouga Desert tours 4 Days:


Day 1 : Marrakech - Ait Benhaddou - Dades gorges :

Departure from Marrakech, Tizi-n-Tichka’s pass (2.260 mt) and road to Ait Benhaddou; berber villages on the way. Arrival to Ait Benhaddou and visit the Kasbah and Lunch. Passing Skoura palm trees valley via Ouarzazate . Visit ting the magnificent kasbah of Amridil. Then we will head off towards the Roses’ valley at many berber villages. Dades gorges through Boumalne. Arriving to the gorges and meeting with local people and their herds. Overnight stay in hotel by the gorges of Dades.

Day 2 : Dades gorges - Todra gorges - Erfoud - Merzouga :

After breakfast we will be departing for Todra gorge, one hour away along Tinghire valley. We will be heading towards Merzouga through Tinjdad, Erfoud and its famous fossilizes marbles and Rissani, the ancient capital of Tafilalet and Merzouga desert. Once we get at the feet of the dunes we will start riding Camels to reach the bivouac at the center of Erg Chebbi dunes. Then we enjoy the sunset with lovely Berber mint tea. Dinner in the camp and nigh in Nomad Tent.

Day 3 : Merzouga - Draa valley - Ouarzazate :

Morning departure on 4X4 WD through Rissani and the immense Bougafer Mountain. Lunch in N’Kob (first village on the way). Afternoon excursion on the one-thousand- kasbahs road (alongside the trail to enjoy the best part of Draa river valley). Arrival in Ouarzazate in the late evening. Dinner and overnight stay in hotel

Day 4 : Ouarzazate - Taourirt - Marrakech :

Breakfast and departure from Ouarzazate : visit the Kasbah of Taourirt and the cinema studios. Head off to the village of I Merzgane and the village of Ighrem. Lunch break in the middle of mountain and way back to Marrakech across Tizi-n-Tichka’s pass. Arriving bet late to Marrakech.
